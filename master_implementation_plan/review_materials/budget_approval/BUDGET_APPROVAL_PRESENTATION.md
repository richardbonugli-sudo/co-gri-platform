## COST JUSTIFICATION

### Why These Resources?

**Full-Stack Engineer (160 hours, $16,000)**
- **Justification:** Core development work across all 4 phases
- **Alternatives Considered:** 
  - Offshore team: Lower cost but higher risk, communication overhead
  - Part-time: Would extend timeline to 8+ weeks, miss market window
- **Decision:** Full-time senior engineer ensures quality and speed

**Data Analyst (20 hours, $1,600)**
- **Justification:** Expert needed to parse and validate CedarOwl forecast data
- **Alternatives Considered:**
  - Engineer does it: Would add 30+ hours to engineer's workload
  - Skip validation: High risk of data quality issues
- **Decision:** Specialist for 1 week ensures data quality

**UI/UX Designer (20 hours, $1,800)**
- **Justification:** 3-tier output structure needs professional design
- **Alternatives Considered:**
  - Engineer designs: Poor UX, longer development time
  - Use existing templates: Doesn't meet unique requirements
- **Decision:** Designer for 2 weeks ensures professional UI

**Product Manager (40 hours, $4,000)**
- **Justification:** Project coordination, stakeholder management, UAT
- **Alternatives Considered:**
  - Engineer self-manages: High risk of scope creep, missed requirements
  - No PM: Communication gaps, misaligned expectations
- **Decision:** Part-time PM ensures project success

**QA Engineer (40 hours, $3,200)**
- **Justification:** Comprehensive testing critical for production quality
- **Alternatives Considered:**
  - Engineer self-tests: Insufficient coverage, bias
  - Automated tests only: Misses UX and integration issues
- **Decision:** Full-time QA in Week 4 ensures quality
